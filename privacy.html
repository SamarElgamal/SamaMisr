International Chemicals Company — Sama Misr ("Sama Misr", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at samaamisr.com or contact us through our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through our contact form (name, company, email, phone, industry, message)

We do not collect any information automatically through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools beyond standard web server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

3. Data Storage & Security

Enquiries submitted through our contact form are processed via Web3Forms and delivered directly to our email inbox. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or maintain a business relationship. If you wish to have your information deleted, please contact us and we will process your request promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted via GitHub Pages. Our contact form is processed via Web3Forms. These services may collect limited technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of their standard operation. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
